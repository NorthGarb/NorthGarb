NorthGarb is an idea that is inspired by the potential for local clothing brands to thrive nationwide due their advantages that they have in the local market. This will be achieved through a superior platform that provides customers with options and includes features like AI outfit matching, viewing clothes off models, clothe an augmented model with an outfit, and offering a wide range of  filter options to simplify shopping i.e. by style, brand, liking. NorthCow aims to provide a smooth and intuitive unique way of shopping that will provide customers with a superior experience resulting in increased user satisfaction and customer loyalty.
